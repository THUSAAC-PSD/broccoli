SumatraPDF
==========

The Broccoli print client bundles and redistributes the SumatraPDF executable as
a separate program, invoked at runtime to print PDFs silently on Windows. It is
not linked into the client and is not a derivative work of it.

Bundled version: 3.5.2
Project home:    https://www.sumatrapdfreader.org/
Source code:     https://github.com/sumatrapdfreader/sumatrapdf
